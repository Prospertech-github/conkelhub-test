Welcome to CONKEL HUB!

At Conkel Hub, we believe that learning should be accessible, flexible, and tailored to your needs. We are an innovative online learning platform that empowers individuals to enhance their tech skills at their own pace and convenience. Whether you're a beginner looking to explore the world of technology or an experienced professional aiming to stay ahead in your field, ULearn provides the tools and resources you need to succeed.

Why Choose Us?

Convenience and Flexibility: With Conkel Hub, you have the freedom to learn anytime, anywhere. No matter if you're a working professional or a busy student, our platform allows you to access high-quality learning materials and courses from the comfort of your home or on-the-go. Take control of your learning journey and study at your own pace.

Diverse Range of Tech Skills: Conkel Hub offers a comprehensive selection of courses covering various None-code tech skills and disciplines. From Product Design, Data Analysis, Business Analysis and Product Management. Expand your knowledge and gain the skills needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving tech industry.

Engaging Learning Experience: Learning should be enjoyable, interactive, and engaging. That's why we prioritise providing a seamless and immersive learning experience. Our courses feature a combination of video lectures, practical exercises, quizzes, and hands-on projects, allowing you to apply what you've learned and build a portfolio of real-world projects.

Expert Instructors: Our courses are created and delivered by industry professionals and subject matter experts who have extensive experience in their respective fields. You can learn from the best as they guide you through each lesson, share valuable insights, and provide practical tips to help you grasp complex concepts more effectively.

Supportive Learning Community: Learning is not just about studying in isolation; it's about being part of a community. At Conkel Hub, you'll have the opportunity to connect with fellow learners through discussion forums, peer-to-peer collaboration, and networking events. Share your ideas, seek help, and gain inspiration from a supportive community of like-minded individuals.

Continuous Learning and Progress Tracking: We believe in lifelong learning, and our platform is designed to support your continuous growth. Track your progress, earn certificates upon course completion, and showcase your achievements to employers or clients. Stay updated with the latest industry trends and ensure you're always equipped with the skills in high demand.

How we started

Our Story

In the heart of innovation, where opportunities and dreams intersect, ConKel Hub was born. Our journey began with a profound vision and a driving passion to transform individuals to skilled talents in Africa and in general improve the education landscape.


Why We Started

Africa is a continent brimming with talent and potential. However, the persistent challenge of a lack of qualified technical expertise is a hurdle that couldn't be ignored.

It became clear that a gap existed between the immense potential of African youth and the global opportunities available to them. This gap sparked the inception of ConKel Hub. Our founders recognised the need to empower young Africans with high-quality technical education.

They believed that knowledge should be accessible to all, regardless of geographical boundaries or socioeconomic backgrounds. This belief fueled their determination to start a company to bridge this gap and empower African students to excel in technology.

How we plan to Bridge the gap

At ConKel Hub, we are committed to redefining the ed-tech landscape in Africa. Our mission is clear: to provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality technical education to aspiring learners across the continent. We've harnessed the power of technology to make education available at your fingertips.

Our approach is built on three pillars:

Quality Content: We curate and create content at par with global standards. Our courses are designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge they need to thrive in the tech industry.


Accessibility: We believe education should not be limited by geography. That's why our platform is accessible to students from all over Africa. You only need an internet connection to unlock a world of opportunities.


Community: Learning is not a solitary endeavour and is best enjoyed together. We foster a community of learners and mentors, providing a support network that guides students through their educational journey.



Join us in this incredible journey of learning, growth, and transformation. Together, we can bridge the gap, empower the future, and redefine what's possible for Africa.
